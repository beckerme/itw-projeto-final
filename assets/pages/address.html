Nossa História

Founded in 1987, MAIZ was born out of the desire to create a space where people could gather to enjoy good food, good company, and unforgettable moments. Located in the heart of Lisbon, our restaurant combines tradition and innovation to offer a unique culinary experience that reflects the best of Portuguese cuisine.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an extraordinary gastronomic experience that goes beyond a simple meal. We believe that each dish tells a story, and it is this story that we want to share with our customers. With an unwavering commitment to quality, we use only the best ingredients, many of them local and seasonal, to ensure freshness and flavor in every bite.

Our values

  • Quality: We are committed to excellence in everything we do. From the selection of ingredients to the preparation of dishes and customer service, quality is our number one priority.
  • Tradition and Innovation: We value our culinary roots and traditions, but we also love to innovate and experiment with new flavors and techniques to surprise and delight our customers.
  • Sustainability: We believe in doing our part to protect the environment. We strive to use sustainable practices in our kitchen, including reducing food waste and choosing suppliers who share our ecological values.
  • Community: [Restaurant Name] is more than just a place to eat – it is a community gathering point. We are proud to support local events and create a space where everyone feels welcome.
